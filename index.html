Controls:

Right Click: Saves the current frame as a PNG image.

Left Click: Clears the canvas, creating a fresh background.

Middle Click: Resets the particle system, generating a new flow field.

Space Key: Creates a new flow field with new random variables.

Mouse Position:

X-axis: Controls the noise scale, affecting the randomness of particle movement.

Y-axis: Controls the noise strength, determining the intensity of the noise field.
